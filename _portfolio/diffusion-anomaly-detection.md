---
title: "Diffusion Residual Fields for Cross-Domain Anomaly Detection"
excerpt: "Residual evolution modeling for robust anomaly signals under domain shift."
collection: portfolio
---

This research project studies how reverse diffusion residuals evolve differently for normal variation and anomaly-related structure. The central idea is that normal variation tends to be absorbed during denoising, while anomaly-aligned residual signals preserve non-stationary drift.

The framework builds field-space features from residual gradients, directional shifts, and path-integral drift. It then aligns source and target domains through field-level statistics, improving cross-domain reuse when target anomalies are unavailable.
